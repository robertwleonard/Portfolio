A Little About Me

With more than twenty years of programming under my belt, I decided it was time to look into a professional career in development. During college, I ran my own part time web design business where I created dozens of websites. But I did not like the ownership side of working in the business and let that convince me it was not the career path for me. I received my degree in English and pushed programming to the hobby side of my life. After deciding to teach programming to students at my school, I began to realize how much I missed it. Now I am looking to start a new career as a web developer. It is exciting to be learning all the new technologies that emerge every day.
